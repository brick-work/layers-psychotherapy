Services

We work with individuals,
couples, and families

Some come for a single session and leave with exactly what they needed. Others are here for the long haul. Both are welcome.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions to explore what's keeping you stuck, understand yourself more clearly, and build toward the life you want. We work at your pace, with your goals in mind.

Anxiety ADHD Trauma Identity Life transitions Depression

Couples Therapy

Helping partners communicate more clearly, repair what's been strained, and build a relationship that works for both of you. We make room for both sides of the story.

Communication Conflict Intimacy Trust Premarital

Family Therapy

Working with the whole system — parents, kids, and the dynamics between everyone — to help your family find steadier ground and communicate in ways that actually work.

Parenting Adolescents Dynamics Reconnection
