I'm Jack Hambrick, a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist in Columbus, Ohio.

I've spent the last ten years studying how people change: in classrooms, in training rooms, and sitting across from the people actually doing the changing. The license is newer than the obsession. What that decade taught me is that most problems that look individual are actually relational: they live in the patterns between you and the people who matter to you. That's where I work.

My approach is collaborative and deliberate. Before we dig in, we build a shared framework: what we're working on, how we'll know it's working, and what “done” looks like. You're not a passenger here. Then we do the work: sometimes brief and targeted, sometimes long and deep. Both are welcome.

"If you're someone who thinks carefully about things, you're in the right place."
