## Who Layers Psychotherapy Is For

Layers is for people who are ready to do real work.

Whether you're at a crossroads, carrying something heavy, or simply aware that something needs to change... this must be the place, for you. I work with individuals, couples, and families navigating relationships, identity, trauma, ADHD, and everything in between.

Some people come for a single session and leave with exactly what they needed. Others are here for the long haul — ready to go deep, peel back layers, and do the kind of sustained work that creates lasting change. Both are welcome here.

Let's build.

Before we begin, we'll establish a shared understanding of what we're working on and how we're going to work on it. Together we will build a framework and come up with a gameplan on how to do it together.

If you're someone who thinks carefully about things, you're in the right place.
